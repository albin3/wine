The Unicef Tap Project challenges you to see how long you can go without something far less vital than clean water – your cell phone. Together people have gone over 200 million minutes without their phones to help children in need of clean water. Time donations are now inactive, but you can still go without your phone and donate to support the UNICEF Tap Project.

Sponsors & Supporters

National Sponsor

Now in its ﬁfth year of support, from March 1-31, 2014 Giorgio Armani Fragrances donated $5 for each Acqua di Giò and Acqua di Gioia fragrance purchased in the U.S. plus the funding equivalent of one day of water for a child in need every 10 minutes users went without their phone.

*For complete sponsorship info please visit uniceftapproject.org on your desktop.

Time Sponsor

UNICEF's Next Generation, a diverse group of young professionals with a shared commitment to UNICEF's future, joins the UNICEF Tap Project this year. The group fundraised and donated funding equivalent to one day of water for a child in need every minute users went without their phone.*

Thank you to all the UNICEF Tap Project supporters whose generous donations are helping provide clean water to the world’s most vulnerable children.**

*For complete sponsorship info please visit uniceftapproject.org on your desktop.
